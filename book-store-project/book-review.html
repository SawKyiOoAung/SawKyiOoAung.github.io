Description

From Here to the Great Unknown is a memoir by Lisa Marie Presley, completed by her daughter Riley Keough after Lisa Marie's passing. It offers an intimate exploration of Lisa Marie's life, from her iconic upbringing as the daughter of Elvis Presley to her struggles with grief, addiction, and personal identity. The book delves into her experiences navigating fame, family legacy, and the challenges that shaped her as a person. Riley Keough faithfully transcribed and expanded upon Lisa Marie's recordings, adding depth to her mother's reflections. The memoir stands out for its raw honesty, detailing moments of vulnerability and resilience. Readers gain insights into Lisa Marie's relationships, motherhood, and her attempts to overcome personal demons. It's not just a story of struggle but also of determination and self-discovery. The book has been celebrated for its heartfelt narrative and emotional resonance. Released in October 2024, it quickly gained critical acclaim, becoming an Oprah's Book Club selection and topping bestseller lists WIKIPEDIA OPRAH.COM.
