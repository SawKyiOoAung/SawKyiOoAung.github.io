Biography

Dr. Phyo Paing is a prominent business trainer, consultant, and advocate for personal development and entrepreneurship in Myanmar. He earned his doctorate degree and later pursued an MBA, equipping himself with the knowledge to inspire others. As the founder of the Young Entrepreneurship Center (YEC), he played a pivotal role in fostering the entrepreneurial spirit among youth, although the center is no longer active. His commitment to enhancing efficiency and promoting mental well-being is reflected in his influential YouTube channel, where he shares insights on personal growth and success.


Contributions and Initiatives

In addition to his educational contributions, Dr. Phyo Paing is the author of two impactful books: 'လူတွေမမှတ်မိအောင် တိုးတက်ပြောင်းလဲလိုက်ပါ' and 'မပြိုင်ပဲနိုင်အောင်လုပ်ပါ' , which guide readers toward achieving their goals without competition. He also created platforms like Toetet for online learning, Akyanpay for financial solutions, and InsiderNote, which facilitates knowledge sharing and community building. These initiatives demonstrate his dedication to empowering individuals and businesses. Through his various projects, Dr. Phyo Paing continues to inspire countless individuals to embrace lifelong learning and unlock their full potential.
